Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 2025  ·  Docolco LLC, United States

← Back to Store

Docolco LLC ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information when you shop at our online store.

1. Information We Collect

When you place an order or contact us, we may collect the following information:

  • Name — to address your order and communications
  • Email address — to send order confirmations and receipts
  • Phone number (WhatsApp) — to coordinate delivery or pickup after purchase
  • Shipping address — if provided, to facilitate delivery
  • Order details — products purchased, quantities, and amounts

We do not collect or store your credit or debit card numbers. All payment data is handled exclusively by Stripe (see Section 4).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Contact you via WhatsApp or email to coordinate pickup or shipping
  • Send order receipts and confirmations through Stripe
  • Respond to questions, inquiries, or support requests
  • Improve our products and services

We do not use your information for automated marketing without your consent, and we do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

3. Order Tracking and Communication

After a successful purchase, our team assigns a unique Order ID in the format DCO-YYYYMMDD-HHMM. This ID is included in your Stripe receipt and is used internally to locate and manage your order. We may follow up via WhatsApp or email using the contact information you provided at checkout.

4. Stripe Payment Processing

All payments are processed securely by Stripe, Inc. Docolco LLC never directly receives or stores your payment card details. When you pay, your card information is transmitted directly to Stripe over an encrypted SSL connection.

Stripe operates under its own Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review it at: stripe.com/privacy.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes. We may share information only:

  • With Stripe to process your payment
  • With shipping carriers if you choose carrier delivery (name and address only)
  • As required by law or to comply with legal obligations

6. Data Retention

We retain order-related information for a reasonable period to fulfill orders, handle returns, and comply with applicable legal obligations. You may request deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us (see Section 9).

7. Cookies

Our website may use essential browser storage (localStorage) to maintain your shopping cart session. We do not use third-party tracking cookies or advertising pixels.

8. Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information. Our website uses HTTPS (SSL encryption) for all communications. Payment processing is delegated entirely to Stripe, which maintains PCI-DSS compliance.

9. California Residents — Your CCPA Rights

If you are a resident of California, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) grants you the following rights:

  • Right to Know: You may request details about the personal information we have collected about you, the sources, the purposes, and the third parties we share it with.
  • Right to Delete: You may request that we delete the personal information we have collected from you, subject to certain legal exceptions.
  • Right to Opt-Out: We do not sell personal information. However, you have the right to direct us not to sell your data should our practices change.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of these rights.

To submit a CCPA request, contact us at info@docolco.com with the subject line "CCPA Request". We will respond within 45 days.

10. Children's Privacy

Our store is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of our store after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us
