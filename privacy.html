Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2025

Docolco LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates this website. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

When you place an order we collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • WhatsApp / phone number
  • Shipping address (if provided)

We do not collect or store credit card data of any kind. All payment information is entered directly on Stripe's secure servers — it never passes through our website.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and confirm your order
  • To contact you via WhatsApp within 24 hours to coordinate delivery or warehouse pickup
  • To send an order confirmation receipt by email (handled by Stripe)
  • To respond to customer service inquiries

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Payment Processing — Stripe

🔒 When you click "Proceed to Stripe — Secure Payment", you are redirected to a checkout page hosted by Stripe, Inc. Your card number, expiration date, and CVV are entered directly on Stripe's PCI-compliant servers. Docolco LLC never sees, receives, or stores this data.

Stripe's privacy policy: stripe.com/privacy

4. Data Retention

Order information is retained for up to 3 years for accounting and legal compliance. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us (see Section 7).

5. Cookies

This website does not use tracking or advertising cookies. Stripe may use cookies on their hosted checkout page, governed by their own privacy policy.

6. Data Security

Our website is served over HTTPS. Payments are processed exclusively through Stripe, which holds PCI DSS Level 1 certification — the highest level of payment security certification available.

7. California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect about you
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of the sale of your information — we do not sell your data

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@docolco.com.

8. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests:

  • Email: info@docolco.com
  • Wholesale inquiries: wholesale@docolco.com
  • Business: Docolco LLC — United States

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
