Shipping & Returns

Effective date: June 2025  ·  Docolco LLC, United States

← Back to Store

At Docolco LLC, shipping and pickup are coordinated personally after your purchase. This page explains how the process works, your delivery options, order tracking, and how to request a return.

1. How It Works

After your payment is confirmed through Stripe, our team reaches out to coordinate the next steps. Here's what to expect:

1
Pay securely at checkout Complete your order through our Stripe-powered checkout. You'll receive an email receipt and a unique Order ID immediately.
2
We contact you within 24 hours Our team will reach out via WhatsApp using the phone number you provided, to confirm your order and discuss delivery or pickup.
3
Choose your delivery option Select warehouse pickup (free) or arrange shipping with a carrier of your choice.
4
Receive your order Pick up at our warehouse or receive the order via your chosen carrier. Use your Order ID for any follow-up.

2. Delivery Options

📦 Warehouse Pickup

Pick up your order directly at our U.S. warehouse at no additional cost. Our team will confirm the address and available time slots via WhatsApp.

🚚 Customer-Arranged Shipping

You select and pay for your preferred carrier (e.g., UPS, FedEx, USPS). We prepare your order for carrier pickup. Shipping costs are the customer's responsibility.

Docolco LLC does not currently offer built-in carrier dispatch at checkout. All logistics are coordinated via WhatsApp after payment is confirmed.

3. Delivery Times

Delivery timelines depend on your selected option:

  • Warehouse pickup: Available once our team confirms readiness (typically 1–2 business days after payment)
  • Customer-arranged shipping: Depends on your chosen carrier and destination. Standard U.S. delivery typically takes 2–7 business days

Docolco LLC is not responsible for delays caused by third-party carriers, weather events, or other circumstances outside our control.

4. Order Tracking

Each order is assigned a unique Order ID at the time of payment:

DCO-20250601-1432

This ID appears on your Stripe receipt and on the order confirmation screen. Use this ID to:

  • Reference your order in WhatsApp conversations with our team
  • Submit a return or refund request
  • Follow up by email at info@docolco.com

For carrier-shipped orders, the shipping tracking number is provided by your chosen carrier directly.

5. Returns

We want you to be happy with your purchase. If there is an issue with your order, please follow these steps:

  • Contact us at info@docolco.com within 7 days of receiving your order
  • Include your Order ID and a clear description of the issue (e.g., wrong item, damaged product)
  • Products must be unopened and in original, undamaged packaging to qualify for a return
  • You may also reach us via WhatsApp if you prefer

Once your return request is approved, we will provide instructions for returning the product. Return shipping costs are the customer's responsibility unless the return is due to our error.

6. Refunds

Approved refunds are processed through Stripe and returned to the original payment method. Please allow 5–10 business days for the refund to appear on your statement, depending on your bank or card issuer.

We reserve the right to deny return requests that do not meet the conditions described above.

7. Damaged or Incorrect Orders

If you receive a damaged or incorrect product, please contact us immediately at info@docolco.com with your Order ID and a photo of the issue. We will resolve the matter promptly at no cost to you.

8. Contact Us

For any shipping, pickup, or return questions:

  • WhatsApp: provided at checkout coordination
